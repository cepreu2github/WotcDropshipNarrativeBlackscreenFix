[h1][b]OVERVIEW[/b][/h1]
Mod fixes after/before mission dropship narrative at blackscreen without subtitles 
and video visible. Now this narrative plays after loading fully complete and all UI
is visible.

[h1][b]COMPATIBILITY[/b][/h1]
Overrides UINarrativeMgr
